# Puzzle 4 Explanations

Here are the answers and explanations for the 15 riddles found in `Puzzle 4.docx`:

**1. A thief in ancient times was caught stealing from the king. The usual punishment for the crime was death but the thief begged for mercy. The king decided to be lenient and let the thief choose his own way to die. Which way did the thief choose?**
* **Answer:** Old age.
* **Explanation:** By choosing to die of old age, the thief cleverly secured a long life instead of an immediate execution.

**2. It can't be seen, its owners don't use it and everything has one.**
* **Answer:** A name.
* **Explanation:** You can't physically see a name (unless written). You rarely use your own name in conversation, but other people use it to refer to you. Everything (objects, people, concepts) has a name.

**3. I'm in a zoo, But not in a jungle, I rarely come in pairs, But I'm in every puzzle. Some think I'm in a xylophone, But, I most certainly am not, You don't see me with a loan, But very strangely in a zealot. I'm in the magical prison of Azkaban. Well, can't you see? I'm in the buzz of a fan. But, my oh my what could I be?**
* **Answer:** The letter 'Z'.
* **Explanation:** This is a letter riddle:
    * Present in "**z**oo" but not "jungle".
    * Present twice in "pu**zz**le".
    * Some think the 'Z' sound is in xylophone, but it's spelled with an 'X'.
    * Not in "loan", but in "**z**ealot".
    * In "A**z**kaban" and "bu**zz**".

**4. It's been around for millions of years, but it's no more than a month old. What is it?**
* **Answer:** The moon.
* **Explanation:** The moon is billions of years old astronomically, but its lunar cycle (from "new moon" to "full moon" and back) takes exactly one month.

**5. "At night they come without being fetched, And by day they are lost without being stolen."**
* **Answer:** Stars.
* **Explanation:** They naturally appear in the night sky and disappear during the day due to the sun's brightness, not because they are taken away.

**6. "I never was, am always to be, No one ever saw me, nor ever will. And yet I am the confidence of all. To live and breathe on this terrestrial ball."**
* **Answer:** Tomorrow (or the future).
* **Explanation:** Tomorrow never physically exists in the present ("never was", "always to be"). Once it arrives, it becomes "today". Yet, everyone confidently plans for and expects it.

**7. "The beginning of eternity. The end of time and space. The beginning of every end, And the end of every place."**
* **Answer:** The letter 'e'.
* **Explanation:** Look at the spelling of the words:
    * **e**ternity (beginning)
    * tim**e** and spac**e** (end)
    * **e**nd (beginning)
    * plac**e** (end)

**8. Oft I must strive with wind and wave, Battle them both when under the sea. I feel out the bottom, a foreign land. In lying still I am strong in the strife; If I fail in that they are stronger than I, And wrenching me loose, soon put me to rout. They wish to capture what I must keep. I can master them both if my grip holds out, If the rocks bring succor and lend support, Strength in the struggle. Ask me my name!**
* **Answer:** An anchor.
* **Explanation:** An anchor rests at the bottom of the sea holding the ship steady. If its grip on the rocks holds, it defeats the wind and waves. If it breaks loose, the ship is pushed away.

**9. When you call my name, I will be gone. What is my name?**
* **Answer:** Silence.
* **Explanation:** As soon as you speak (call a name), you break the silence.

**10. The one who sees me, that's me. Who am I?**
* **Answer:** A reflection (or a mirror).
* **Explanation:** When you look at your reflection, the person looking back at you is yourself.

**11. I am what I am, but if you know what I am, I am no longer what I am. Guess, what am I?**
* **Answer:** A surprise (or a secret).
* **Explanation:** The essence of a surprise is that it's unknown. Once you figure out what the surprise is, it ceases to be a surprise.

**12. What goes up, but doesn't go down?**
* **Answer:** Your age.
* **Explanation:** You continually get older, but you never get younger.

**13. A woman lived in an apartment building on the seventh floor. Every morning she took the lift to the ground floor and went to her work. But when she came home, she took the lift to the fourth floor and then continued by walking the stairs up. Why would she do that?**
* **Answer:** She is very short (or a little person).
* **Explanation:** She can only reach high enough on the elevator button panel to press the button for the 4th floor. 

**14. What does not have weight but is able to let a ship sink?**
* **Answer:** A hole.
* **Explanation:** A hole is just an empty space (so it weighs nothing), but it allows water into the ship, causing it to sink.

**15. Who makes it, doesn't tell it. Who gets it, doesn't know it. Who knows what it is, doesn't want it. What is it?**
* **Answer:** Counterfeit money.
* **Explanation:** The person printing fake money keeps it a secret. The person receiving it usually thinks it's real. But if someone knows it is counterfeit, they won't accept it because it is worthless and illegal.
